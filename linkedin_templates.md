🛰️ Monitoring Water Quality from Space

I've just published my latest remote sensing project: tracking chlorophyll-a concentrations in Welsh waterbodies using satellite imagery.

🔬 The Challenge:
Inland lakes are vital water resources but vulnerable to pollution and algal blooms. Traditional monitoring is expensive and limited in coverage.

💡 The Solution:
Using Sentinel-2 satellite data (10m resolution) and the Living Wales datacube infrastructure, I developed an automated monitoring system that:

✅ Detects chlorophyll concentrations using NDCI (Normalized Difference Chlorophyll Index)
✅ Provides time-series analysis to track seasonal patterns
✅ Maps spatial distribution of chlorophyll across waterbodies
✅ Enables early detection of algal blooms

🛠️ Tech Stack:
• Python & Open Data Cube framework
• Sentinel-2 satellite imagery (ESA)
• xarray for multi-dimensional data analysis
• Living Wales ARD infrastructure

📊 Results:
Successfully tracked water quality trends in Llangorse Lake with 5-day temporal resolution, demonstrating how satellite remote sensing can complement traditional monitoring.

This approach is scalable to all Welsh waterbodies and demonstrates the power of combining Earth observation with cloud computing.

## Tips for Maximum Engagement

### Timing
- Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM
- Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons

### Hashtags
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular (#Python, #DataScience) with niche (#RemoteSensing, #WaterQuality)
- Place at the end of post

### Visuals
- Include an image: NDCI map, time-series plot, or RGB lake image
- Infographics perform well
- Before/after comparisons are engaging

### Engagement Strategies
- Ask a question at the end
- Tag relevant organizations (if appropriate)
- Respond to all comments within first hour
- Share in relevant groups

### Follow-up Posts
- Behind-the-scenes: "How I built this"
- Technical deep-dive: "The science behind NDCI"
- Results showcase: "What the data revealed"
- Lessons learned: "Challenges I faced"

---

Choose the template that best matches your personal brand and the audience you want to reach!
